Who We Are

Thrive Together Initiative is a registered community-based organization in Uganda. We empower women, youth, and persons with disabilities through entrepreneurship, financial literacy, and skills development building sustainable pathways out of poverty.

About us

We are a legally registered Community-Based Organization (Reg. No. MKN/CBO/5064/2025) serving communities in Kyampisi Sub-County, Mukono Municipality, and Namataba Town Council in Mukono District, Uganda.
We are currently running the Magic Hands Initiative which empowers marginalized rural populations, particularly youth, women, and persons with disabilities (PWDs), by strengthening their entrepreneurial and business development skills through financial literacy, entrepreneurship training, digital literacy, business mentorship, and value-added skills training.

  • What make us different ?


    1. Inclusive by Design: We intentionally reach those often left behind, i.e., rural women, PWDs, and out-of-school youth, creating opportunities where few exist.

    2. Hands-on & Localized: Training is practical, culturally relevant, and accessible within the communities we serve.

    3. End-to-End Support: We go beyond training by offering business mentorship, market access, and a safe innovation space that supports long-term growth.

    4. Community-Owned Solutions: Our programs are designed with the community, not for them, ensuring ownership, relevance, and lasting impact.

    • Discover our Programs and Activities

    • Entrepreneurship & Business Skills Training.

        We have empowered 248 individuals, including youth, women, and persons with disabilities, through practical entrepreneurship and business skills training. Our sessions go beyond theory, equipping participants with hands-on value-addition techniques and foundational knowledge to start and grow sustainable enterprises. Each trainee leaves not just with skills, but with renewed confidence and a real pathway to economic independence.

      • Business Mentorship & Coaching

          We are actively building partnerships with local experts and professionals to launch a robust business mentorship program tailored for grassroots entrepreneurs. This initiative will connect aspiring business owners with seasoned mentors, offering guidance on strategy, operations, and sustainable growth. Our first mentorship cohort is set to launch in August 2025, creating a valuable platform for real-world learning, accountability, and entrepreneurial success.

        • Market Linkages & Product Exhibitions

            We have successfully participated in two market linkage events this year, providing our beneficiaries with the opportunity to showcase their products, gain real-time customer feedback, and learn from other entrepreneurs. These events not only increased their visibility but also offered invaluable insights into product improvement, packaging, and market trends, bridging the gap between small-scale producers and larger markets.

          Future Plans


          We plan to establish an Entrepreneurs’ Safe Space in Kabembe to help young people turn ideas into sustainable businesses with free tools, training, and mentorship.

          We will also launch a revolving microcredit facility, offering small, low-barrier loans to women, youth, and persons with disabilities.

          Both initiatives aim to reduce start-up costs, foster innovation, and create lasting opportunities for grassroots entrepreneurs.
